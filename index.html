What goes into the Vehicle Census?

Vehicle registrations, inspection records,  EPA fuel economy ratings, and census data make up the Census itself. Annual mileage estimates are based on odometer readings from successive annual safety inspections and matched to geocoded registration addresses.

Learn more

What is the Vehicle Census used for?

Massachusetts Vehicle Census provides a mechanism for setting informed targets for transportation-related GHG reduction and tracking progress toward those targets at municipal or sub-municipal level on things like CAFE standards and smart growth land use package.

Vehicle Miles Traveled (per household)

In 2014, households across MA traveled less than 20 miles per day in Inner Core and over 100 miles per day in some remote suburbs and rural towns. Statewide, households traveled ~48 miles per household per day

Fuel Efficiency

“Effective” Miles per Gallon of MA's passenger vehicle fleet (weighted by miles driven of each vehicle) has improved 1.5 miles per gallon since 2009, even as vehicle fleet has aged.
